Problems are Infuriating!

They disrupt our plans,
delay progress and
create additional work.

Are you discouraged by a
situation but don't know
how to change it?

Do you spend time reworking
things others have made instead
of creating something original?

Have you developed things
without understanding the
actual problem then been
frustrated by the results?

Problems are an integral part of life

Despite the frustration they cause, they can
teach us important lessons, which can be
applied across different areas of our lives.

Are you ready to
break from the pack?

Story-building solves puzzling problems.

Stories capture attention and arouse curiosity.


When puzzling problems are depicted as a story, it
creates a panoramic view of the problem landscape.


Creating a physical story enables you to visualize
problem complexity, which eliminates ambiguity
and deepens understanding.


Story-building enables you to think diffferently and see
things you previously weren't aware of, producing a
kaleidoscope of ideas you never knew existed.
